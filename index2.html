Hey You ❤️

I don’t know the exact moment you became my favorite person… but somewhere between the late-night talks and the quiet comfort, you did.

You didn’t just walk into my life — you settled into my heart like you were always meant to be there.

With you, everything feels different. Safer. Softer. Brighter.

You have this beautiful way of calming my chaos without even trying.

When I overthink, you reassure. When I doubt, you remind me. When I feel low, you lift me — gently.

Loving you doesn’t feel dramatic or complicated. It feels peaceful. It feels right.

I adore the little things about you — your smile, your patience, the way you care in ways most people wouldn’t even notice.

You don’t just hold my hand. You hold my heart carefully, like it matters.

If life ever gets loud or uncertain, I hope you remember this —

There is someone out here who feels incredibly lucky to call you theirs.

And that someone… is me. ❤️

Always choosing you. Always.

I love you ❤️
