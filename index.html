Dear Sister,

This is not a special occasion. Not a festival. Not a reminder.

Just a quiet little note for someone very special in my life — YOU.

I may not always say it, but I truly feel lucky to have you as my sister.

Through childhood fights, silly arguments, shared secrets, and endless memories — you have always been there.

You are strong in ways you don’t even realize. You care deeply. You give so much love without expecting anything in return.

No matter how busy life gets or where we both go in the future, one thing will never change — I will always be there for you.

Whenever you feel tired, confused, or unsure… remember that you are capable, brave, and incredibly important.

You deserve happiness. You deserve peace. You deserve all the success coming your way.

And if the world ever feels heavy, just know you have a sibling who believes in you completely.

I’m proud of you. Always.

With lots of love ❤️

Your Forever Partner in Crime
